Loving, Learning, Living represents the emotions felt inside the novel, The Education of Cyrus. Play as Persian solider under the honorable leadership of Cyrus. Because the theme of the story centers around loyalty, justice, benevolence for his people, this game is representative of the guard's commitment to Cyrus. Having successfully built a strong & multi-dimensional, it's not surprise that the Ancient Persian Empire grew. The compassion and loyalty he inspires in his people created a beautiful world where his army desired to give back. Every jump that you perform represents that strength.
